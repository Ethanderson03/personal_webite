Artifacts of Compression

Finding A Form

The form of my micro internets was inspired by the cover of a book I owned "Scroll Down And Keep Scrolling" by Fiona Banner. I was drawn to the arcitactul nature of the form on its cover and dug into its historical nature as an object to understand it better. After a little bit of googleing I discovered that it was a specific type of coloumn called a font. As a graphic desinger I knew imidiatly that I had to design a font of my own to complete my degree project.

Designing A Font

It was decieded; this form would house my internets. To me it represented a kind of rebirth, and also the begining of some kind of catherdral. I wanted these micro internets to feel spacial. I wanted them to have high vaulted ceilings in some places and become small and cramped in other places. The Font as a form spoke to that perfectly. I initally thought I would model it in 3D and then print it out on a 3D printed but then I felt the urge to ditch the needless technology and mold it out of clay. From there I made a two part mold with the help of my friend Sam Lavoia and filled that mold with plaster. In the end I made 6 Fonts.
